June 20, 2025

Report at the venue to collect your IDs and receive resources. Begin your 48-hour journey of innovation and development.
July 11, 2025 (Afternoon)
Mid-Event Checkpoint
Progress review at halfway point. Mentors will be available to assist with any technical challenges to help achieve your goals.
July 12, 2025 (Afternoon)
Final Submission
Push your final code to the repository with complete documentation in readme.md for running the code. Prepare for demonstrations.
