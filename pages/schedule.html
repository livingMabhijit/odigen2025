Event Schedule

May 1-15, 2025
Phase 1: Registration & Team Formation
Submit your application and form your team. Each team should have 3-4 members with diverse skills in AI, development, and problem-solving.
May 20-25, 2025
Phase 2: Online Qualification Round
Teams will participate in online challenges to demonstrate their technical capabilities and innovative thinking in AI/ML problem-solving.
June 1-10, 2025
Phase 3: Project Proposal Submission
Qualified teams submit detailed project proposals outlining their innovative solutions using generative AI technologies.
June 15, 2025
Phase 4: Proposal Evaluation & Selection
Expert panel reviews proposals and selects teams for the final hackathon round based on innovation, feasibility, and impact.
July 5-7, 2025
Phase 5: Final Hackathon Event
48-hour intensive development sprint at IIT Bhubaneswar. Teams build their solutions with mentorship support and present to judges.
July 7, 2025
Phase 6: Presentations & Awards
Final project presentations, demonstrations, and award ceremony celebrating the most innovative solutions.
