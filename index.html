Competition Settings

This hackathon aims to find GenAI practitioners who have the potential to excel in the field of data science, comprising sub-domains including but not limited to software engineering, cybersecurity, and IoT. This competition will provide ample opportunity to showcase your skills and knowledge in the aforementioned areas.

Further challenging our efforts at communicating with the institutions of Odisha to address complex, emergent problems involves teams drawn from multiple disciplines. Our team refers to this type of development as convergent research, which brings together knowledge, tools, and expertise from diverse domains to solve industry-critical challenges. In healthcare, this means integrating clinical insights, data science, and regulatory understanding to improve patient outcomes and streamline care delivery. In IoT, it involves combining embedded systems, connectivity, and analytics to enable smart, interoperable solutions across devices and environments. In cybersecurity, convergent research aligns threat intelligence, risk modeling, and system architecture to build robust, adaptive defense mechanisms. This approach accelerates the translation of ideas into deployable technologies with real-world impact.
